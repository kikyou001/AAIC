Who We Are

A group of professionals inside and outside Algeria with a clear and strong objective to introduce the students to the space and rocket science. To promote the aeronautic domain across the country through a competition and accompany and expose student to international contest and competitions.

Promoting excellence, this year, the 3 firsts teams will participate in the World's largest University Rocket Engineering Competition. competing against international universities in USA.

Define

The third edition 2019 will be held at Blida University on December 2 nd and 3 rd 2019.

Design and Build

Students are called to follow a cahier de charge requesting design and building a rocket. No launching is authorized yet. Two category is available (reaching altitude of 10 k with weight constraint or 30 k feet)

Compete (national -- No launching)

All participants will expose their design and will be evaluated by judges how meeting the requirement defined by the cahier de charge. Only selected teams will presenting the details of their design the day after. Among the selected team, the top three will be declared winner of 2019 edition.

Compete and Launch (international)

The Winner (top 3) will be accompanied to participate to Spaceport America Cup in June 2020 at New Mexico, USA.

What We Do / What we need / Why you are important

Supervise students through an engineering project.

More than 30 professors and professionals to supervise hundreds of student, by providing courses, documentations and materials. Improve their knowledge on aeronautics and space field. Complete an engineering project by building a rockets, and hopefully launching with a great success on international gathering. Build their skills working as team. Compete and participate to international competitions.
